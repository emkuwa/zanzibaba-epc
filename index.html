About Us

Company Introduction

Building Tanzania's future through engineering excellence and construction innovation

Engineering Excellence Since Inception

Zanzibaba EPC is a premier Engineering, Procurement and Construction company headquartered in Zanzibar, Tanzania. We provide comprehensive EPC solutions including Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Project Management and Supply Chain Solutions across Mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar.

Our integrated approach combines engineering design, procurement logistics, construction execution, project management and supply chain solutions to deliver projects for industrial, commercial, hospitality, infrastructure, irrigation and development sectors.

With a team of qualified engineers, project managers, and construction professionals, we support government institutions, international organizations, private developers, and industrial clients with reliable, on-time, and within-budget project delivery.
